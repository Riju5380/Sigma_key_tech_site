Account & Data Deletion

Complying with Google Play Console policies to let you manage your student safety monitoring profile.

Under the Google Play Developer Account Deletion Policy, if you have an account created in the Sigma Key Tech mobile app (developed by Astfast Infotech Pvt Ltd), you have the right to request deactivation of your mobile login and deletion of app-cached synchronization profiles.
