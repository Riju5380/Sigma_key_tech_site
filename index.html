Features

Designed with Parent Peace of Mind in Mind

SIGMA KEY TECH extends the school's attendance portal, giving you access to critical security and informational tools from your smartphone.

Real-Time RFID Entry/Exit

Instantly receive notifications showing exactly when your child enters or leaves the school premises, logged via advanced physical RFID reader gates.

Complete Attendance History

Access a comprehensive calendar and logging log of previous school attendance. Easily identify past attendance patterns and check active verification logs.

Categorized School Notices

Never miss a school update. The dashboard organizes school announcements into 3 visual categories: Academic, Events, and Emergency Alerts.

System Architecture

Secure Portal Continuity

The SIGMA KEY TECH mobile app is a direct extension of your school's attendance management system. The core database and control dashboard are hosted at our secure server domain:

sigmakey.tech

When students or teachers tap their RFID school IDs on physical readers at school checkpoints, the local reader immediately synchronizes the timestamp with the backend server. The app communicates with this server via secure private APIs, fetching the logs and notices immediately for parents.

Account Setup

How Do Parents Gain Access?

To protect student privacy, there are no public sign-ups on the mobile app. All account management is tightly integrated with the school portal.

1

School Enrollment

Your child's school administration registers your email address directly in their database via the school control dashboard at sigmakey.tech.

2

Initial Temporary Login

Once registered, a secure default temporary password is automatically provisioned for your first login. Log into the app using your email and temporary password.

3

Password Update

For security, you will be prompted to update your password immediately within the app. Subsequent logins will securely require your custom password.

Authentication requests are securely routed directly to the primary database server at sigmakey.tech.
