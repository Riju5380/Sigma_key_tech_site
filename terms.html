1. Scope of Service

Sigma Key Tech is an educational monitoring application developed by Astfast Infotech Pvt Ltd and designed for parents and guardians. The App is a direct continuity of the school attendance management software and backend server hosted at sigmakey.tech. It displays real-time student and teacher entry/exit timestamps generated by physical RFID reader gates, compiles attendance histories, and displays school notice board bulletins (Academic, Events, and Emergency categories).

2. Account Access and Credentials

Access to the App is strictly controlled to maintain student security:

  • No Public Registration: Users cannot register or create accounts within the App. Accounts are provisioned exclusively by enrolled school administrators who add parent emails to the secure database at sigmakey.tech.
